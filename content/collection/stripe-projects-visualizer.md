## Why I chose to highlight it

The project visualizes the services and relationships created through Stripe Projects. What caught my attention is that it helps developers understand infrastructure that may have been provisioned by AI agents. By making the architecture visible, it brings greater transparency and trust to AI-assisted decisions and helps developers stay connected to the systems they are building.

What I would also highlight, is that in her blog post about this project, Anna Spies, devrel for Stripe reflects on how it took years by many engineers to create ssuch a visualization tool. Now the time has been compressed. The visualizer is useful, specially in an AI-driven context. It also brings Spies' past expertise into play.
